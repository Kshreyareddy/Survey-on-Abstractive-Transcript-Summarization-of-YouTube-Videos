Abstractive Transcript Summarization of YouTube Videos.

About

Summarizing transcripts of such videos helps us to quickly search for relevant patterns in the video without having to go through the entire content. Abstractive transcript summarization model is very useful in extracting YouTube video transcripts and generates a summarized version. An automatic summarizer's purpose is to shorten the time of reading, enable easier selection, be less prejudiced compared to humans, and portray content that is compressed while preserving the important material of the actual document. Extractive and abstractive approaches are the two most common ways to summarise text. Extractive approaches choose phrases or sentences from input text, whereas Abstractive methods generate new words from input text, making the task much more difficult.
